ABOUT PROBIST

Probist is an easy, simple and proven interface for embedding all YouTube Channel videos onto social media pages as well as custom websites. Users just need to sign-up at Probist which literally takes less than a minute and select the YouTube channel/channels they would like to sync videos from, followed by a click of a button to create their own website.
