ABOUT SUPPORT SYNC

Support Sync is a returns management system that synchronizes customers, support agents, and return center personnel in real-time. It is a low cost cloud based application, which makes it easy for low as well as high scale companies to process their clients returns more efficiently and effectively. They have developed mature, through years of customer satisfaction.
